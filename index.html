Welcome to Harrisonburg!

On this site you will find information about Downtown Harrisonburg and James Madison University. Harrisonburg is a city in the state of Virginia located in the middle of the Shenandoah Valley. The city is home to many different sites and attractions. Harrisonburg is best known for its arts, history, university, and culture.
