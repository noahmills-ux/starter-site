Harrisonburg is known for its vibrant downtown; the first designated Arts & Cultural District and the first Culinary District in Virginia.

Downtown Harrisonburg is a unique area full of shops, attractions, and diverse architecture. The Downtown food is very popular among local redisents and students, and tourists and newcomers often travel to Harrisonburg because of the amazing food. Aside from food, you will find museums, shops, markets, music venues and much more in Downtown Harrisonburg.
