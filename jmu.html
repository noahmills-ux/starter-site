James Madison University is a Liberal Arts College located in Harrisonburg, VA. JMU's student body is around 20,000 students. JMU is strongly rooted in the Harrisonburg community and it's students make up a significant portion of the city's puplation when school is in session.

JMU is regarded as one of the top schools in the nation. JMU was recently reviewed in 2019 as the "Best College for Getting a Job" among all other Virginia Universities. JMU was also reviewed in 2019 to be the number #1 recommended school by students in the United States.
